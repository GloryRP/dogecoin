🌊 Avalanche Effect Explained:

Any small change in the transaction data — sender name, receiver name, amount, or even spacing — creates a completely different hash value. This property is called the Avalanche Effect and helps blockchain detect tampering immediately. In Dogecoin, even changing one byte in a block invalidates the entire chain from that point forward.
